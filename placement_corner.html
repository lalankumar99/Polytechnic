Resume & CV Building Guide

1

Standard Format

Keep it clean and professional. Use a single-page layout for freshers. Include clear sections: Objective, Education, Skills, Projects, and Certifications.

2

Polytechnic Specific Tips

Highlight your practical lab skills, minor/major projects, and industrial training heavily. Employers value hands-on experience in diploma candidates.

3

Action Verbs & Grammar

Use strong action verbs (e.g., Developed, Designed, Managed). Ensure zero grammatical errors as it reflects your communication skills.

Post-Diploma Career Roadmap

Government Jobs (PSUs & State)

Prepare for RRB JE, SSC JE, State Electricity Boards, DRDO, ISRO. Requires dedicated technical and non-technical preparation.

Private Sector Placement

Join MNCs or core manufacturing companies immediately after diploma. Focus on campus drives and walk-in interviews.

Higher Studies (B.Tech LEET)

Opt for Lateral Entry Entrance Test (LEET) to directly enter the 2nd year of B.Tech for rapid career growth.

Apprenticeship Opportunities

Register on NATS portal for 1-year paid apprenticeship in Govt/Private sectors to gain industrial experience.

Skill Development

Learn modern software (AutoCAD, Coding, PLC SCADA) to freelance or start an entrepreneurial journey.
